 Welcome

Hello, My name is William Mannie. I am an avid runner and aspiring pilanthopist whos mission is to to run the world... literally (insert evil laugh). In April of 2012, I set out a goal to run my first marathon in my hometown of Chicago,Il. I partnered with the American Cancer Scoicety, trained all summer, and asked my friends and family for donations. Through their love and support I completed the 2012 Bank of America Chicago Marathon and raised over $1,700 towards cancer research.

Through this experience I gained a substantial amount of mental toughness and deterninatin that left me wanting more. Now, a year later, I realize I want to use my running ability to make a global impact, by running the World Marathon Majors (Tokyo, Boston, London, Berlin, Chicago and New York City) to benefit the less fortunate.

So this year I will be participating in the 2013 ING New York City Marathon. I have partnered with the New York Road Runners' Team For Kids in efforts to impove the lives of youth that have little to no access to regular physical activity. My goal is to raise $2,620. Please support my cause today by dontaing or subscibing to my monthly mailing list.
